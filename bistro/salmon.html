Garlic Salmon

This is one of my favorite recipes for when folks come over for dinner. I've already shared this popular recipe with many of my friens. The best thing is it's so easy to make!

  • 1/4 c. dry sherry
  • 2 T. soy sauce
  • 2 T. vegetable oil
  • 3-4 T. freshly minced garlic
  • 1 to 2 lbs salmon filets
  1. Mix tpgether first 5 ingredients in a shallow dish. Place the cleaned and de-boned salmon filets face down in the mixture and allow to marinate for 1/2 to 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the boiler. Transfer filets to a baking sheet, skin-down. Broil the salmon on the top rack for 7 to 9 minutes, or until done to your liking
  3. I usually serve this with an aromatic rice and a lightly seasoned stir fry
